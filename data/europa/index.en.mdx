# Hygiene and safety

Find following recommendations to maintain hygiene and safety at the Europa building:

- Wash your hands when entering the centre and frequently throughout the day. The access points to the Europa building include hydrogels.
- Maintain a safety distance of 2 meters at all times.
- Mandatory use of a face mask in those spaces where the safety distance of 2m cannot be guaranteed.
- All users of the centre are asked not to enter the building and avoid situations of social contact in the event of any possible symptoms of COVID-19.

# Access points

Please take into account following recommendations while entering the Europa building:

- The use of the elevator is limited to one person. In any case, it is recommended, whether possible, to use the stairs.
- Remember to continually respect the safety distance to the access points to the building, since they are a particularly crowded area.

# Use of common areas

Please follow the instructions below when using the common areas of the Europa building:

- Mandatory use of face masks in common areas.
- It is recommended to reduce face-to-face meetings to the minimum. If this were the case, the safety distance must be maintained at all times and face masks must be used.
- Maximum 1 person in each bathroom. The following bathrooms are assigned:
  - Basement floor: users of the basement floor and visits.
  - It is recommended that companies do not use bathrooms on other floors.

# External visits

Please restrict visits to the minimum strictly necessary, and accompany and inform visitors to ensure that they comply with the indicated safety rules.
